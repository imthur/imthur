## shipping

### unifica gestão

hr and payroll saas for small and mid-sized brazilian companies.
replaces accounting offices and multiple point tools with a single
platform: full brazilian labor-law payroll (progressive inss, legal
add-ons, employer contributions), 13th salary, full vacation module,
6-type termination flow, employee self-service portal, document
management with expiration alerts, schema-per-tenant multi-tenancy
and full audit trail.

calculation depth above industry standard: real progressive tax
tables, proportional notice period (law 12.506/11), 484-a agreement,
tst precedent 449, fgts digital and itemized employer costs on every
payslip.

```
status  in production · currently onboarding first customers
stack   python · django · drf · postgresql · celery · redis · docker · render
```

[→ open unifica gestão](https://unificagestao.com)

across general systems. rag pipeline with hybrid embedding search,
